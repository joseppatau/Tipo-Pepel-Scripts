## Replace Outlines From Master

**Description**
Replaces or copies glyph outlines from a selected source master to all other masters, with an option to store them in background layers.

**Author**
Josep Patau Bellart (with AI assistance)

**License**
Apache2

---

## ⚠️ Warning

This tool can overwrite outlines across multiple masters.
Use with caution or store results in background instead.

---

## Features

* Replace outlines across all masters
* Copy outlines to background instead of replacing
* Copy width from source master
* Works on selected glyphs
* Batch processing

---

## Core

* Master-based outline duplication
* Layer overwrite system
* Background layer support

---

## Scope

* Selected glyphs
* Source master → all other masters

---

## Requirements

* Glyphs App 3.x
* Multiple masters

---

## Usage

1. Select glyphs
2. Choose source master
3. Select mode:

   * Replace outlines
   * Put in background
4. Click **Process Selected Glyphs**

---

## Notes

* Skips glyphs without shapes in source master
* Does not modify source master
* Ideal for syncing or rebuilding masters
