## Multi-Master Preview

**Description**
Displays the current glyph across all master layers side by side, enabling direct visual comparison and interpolation inspection.

**Author**
Josep Patau Bellart (with AI assistance)

**License**
Apache2

---

## Features

* Horizontal layout of all masters
* Real-time updates while editing
* Zoom control with slider and CMD + scroll
* Scrollable view for large glyphs
* Drag navigation (Alt or Space)
* Double-click to open glyph in specific master

---

## Core

* Master-based layer mapping
* Consistent scaling and baseline alignment
* Dynamic layout recalculation
* NSScrollView integration

---

## Scope

* Current glyph
* All master layers

---

## Requirements

* Glyphs App 3.x
* Multi-master font

---

## Usage

1. Open the panel
2. Select a glyph
3. Compare shapes across masters
4. Zoom and scroll as needed
5. Double-click a master to open it

---

## Notes

* Ideal for interpolation debugging
* Helps detect inconsistencies across masters
* Works best with compatible outlines
